Hi, I’m Hosung Kim.

I am a current USC business student with interests in consulting fields. In order to provide data-driven business decisions to clients, I started to explore data in depth, from data cleaning to analysis and visualization.

One thing that makes me immersed into data analysis field is that there are so many opportunities to learn new things. The fact that data analysis can’t be done without gaining knowledge of the specific field makes every process of data analysis fascinating. I look forward to communicate with various people with unique interests to learn basic knowledge of the field and understand data more accurately, which are crucial to solve business problems.

In today’s fast-paced world, all areas are experiencing dramatic changes, which is why predictions of trends become more difficult than before. Therefore, appropriate data analysis could maximize potential profits, whereas inaccurate interpretation of data could hurt business more than we think.

Throughout portfolio projects, I could qualify skills for appropriate and creative data analysis. With experience in spreadsheets, SQL, Python, R, and Tableau, I am skilled for most data analysis steps: data transformation, data cleaning, data analysis, data visualization and results communication.
